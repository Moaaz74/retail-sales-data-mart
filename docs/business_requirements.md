# Business Requirements

## Business Process

Retail Sales

## Business Goal

Provide a data mart that enables analysis of sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and geographical trends.

## Key Business Questions

### Sales Analysis

* What are the top-selling products?
* What are the most profitable products?
* How do sales change over time?

### Customer Analysis

* Which customer types generate the highest revenue?
* Which customers place the most orders?

### Product Analysis

* Which product categories perform best?
* Which products contribute most to profit?

### Geography Analysis

* Which cities generate the highest sales?
* Which states generate the highest profit?

### Shipping Analysis

* Which shipping modes are most frequently used?
* What is the impact of shipping costs on profitability?
